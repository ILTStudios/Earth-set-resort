A Few of Our Resident Plans
Budget Price
The Budget Price of our Hotel ranges from $5k to $10k depending on the amount of extra luggage and the access card bought.
This Budget Price is the lowest out of all our competitors and provides a variety of luxuries that aren't found in this price range.
From Accesability to the Full-Access Card to 3 Course Meals. The Budget Price really is a bang for the Buck for all Residents.
The Drawbacks of the Budget Price however is that your stay can only be awaited till 3 days, any longer and you will have to upgrade.
The Budget Price also gives the Residents a King sized bed upon the top-deck.
Regular Price
The Regular Price of our Resort ranges from $10k to $15k depending on the amount of extra luggage, access card, and your specialitiy of room.
Rooms ranging in all Prices are the same. Singles, Twins, Families, and Luxuries. They range from small single person rooms to large multi person rooms perfect for a gang of 8.
The Regular Price also contains 3 course meals and a complimentary dessert on the first night. You may also avail the ability to order Desserts and Dine at the Snack Bar which serves General Meals at all hours.
The Regular Price has rooms at the medium-deck.
Extravagent Price
Extravagence costs around $30k to $70k depending on your rooms, luggage, access card, and pass.
Extravagence at Earth-Set Resort is achieved in a multitude of ways. From unlimited meals (Orderable from Room Service) to access to the Extravagence Pass (A Pass that grants access to luxury Areas such as parks and Open Decks), and of course to luxury in all rooms.
Extravagence is not only achieved in meals or perks, but also comfort. Extravagence members are given butlers to help move around, and also are given access to the captains quarters where they can (if permitted) to man the Resort.

A Few Meals Dined with at Earth-Set
General Meals
General Meals at the Earth-Set Resort Include Meals That can be bought throughout the day at the Resort.
Meals Ranging from small purchasable snack, drinks, and sweets.
To full on large meals such as roasted chicken Tender Loin, and much more. The menu is heavily restricted when it comes to General Meals because of the increasing price of Travel.
Thus Fruits and Vegetables are often rarely found in the General Meal Category.
A Fine Lunch
Lunches at the Earth-Set Resort Begin at 1:30 PM or 13:30 Hours till 4:00PM or 16:00 Hours.
Lunches include a small array of fruits and vegetables that Residents can enjoy, and a few delights such as Juices, Soda's, and Freshly Squeezed Drinks.
Lunches also include a wide variety of Common Lunch Items such as steamed rice, Gravy's of all types, Curries, Meats, Vegetable Curries, and more.
A Luxury Dinner
Here at the Earth-Set Resort we like to specialise our Dinners, not because of luxury but because of health concerns.
Which is why Dinner is directly supplied to Your room No. Every day at 9:00PM or 21:00 Hours.
The Residents are given a few choices each night to choose from. If you wish you can call beforehand and Dine Outside as well.
Dinners also include more variety of meals that are more specific to a certain culture. Such as Greek Dinners, Chinese Dinners, Indian, Mexican, etc.
A Sweet Dessert
Desserts are an optional add-on Provided by Earth-Set Resort, and do avail extra costs.
Which is why dinner is also a Deliverable/Dine out Meal ordered by the residents themselves.
Sweets from all countries and the classic cakes, ice cream, and pies are just a few out of our selection.
